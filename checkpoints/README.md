# Recipe Share Recovery Checkpoints

These files are fallback copies for the code-heavy Recipe Share labs.

If your starter app gets stuck after a lab, open the checkpoint for that lab and use its contents to replace the matching file in `starter/`.

- Keep your own `starter/fusabase-config.js`. The checkpoint files do not replace your project config.
- Use the checkpoint for the lab you just completed, not the final app, so the later TODOs stay in place.
- Lab 6 and Lab 7 include an `index.html` checkpoint because Lab 6 unhides the photo field.
